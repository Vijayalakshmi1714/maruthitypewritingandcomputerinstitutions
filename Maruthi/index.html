About Us

Welcome to Maruthi Typewriting Institute, your premier destination for excellence in typewriting and computer education. Established in 1987 in Panruti, our institution has grown into a leading center for professional training, offering top-quality coaching with a strong focus on typewriting and COA (Certificate in Office Automation).

With over three decades of experience, we are committed to equipping our students with the knowledge, skills, and job opportunities necessary for success.

Why Choose Us?

  • Expert Trainers – Highly experienced instructors dedicated to student success.
  • Modern Infrastructure – Well-maintained machines and fully equipped classrooms.
  • Comprehensive Courses – We offer:
    • Python, Java
    • C, C++
    • DCA (Diploma in Computer Application)
    • Tally
    • FullStack Development
    • Web Development
  • Student-Centric Approach – Practical learning and real-world applications.
  • Convenient Facilities – Spacious classrooms and a supportive atmosphere.
